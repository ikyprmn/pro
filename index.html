About Me

Hello! I am **Rizky Permana**, a UI/UX Designer who is passionate about creating interfaces that are not only beautiful but also intuitive and easy to use. I believe that good design is the solution to user problems.

I have experience in conducting user research, creating wireframes, prototypes, and implementing responsive designs. My focus is on providing a smooth user experience and delightful interactions.
